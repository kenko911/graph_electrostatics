# units
Throughout this repo we use:
- charge: electron
- length: Angstrom
- energy: electron Volt
- potential: Volt

## electrostatic equations in these units
Poissons equation:

$$
\nabla^2 V = - \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0}
$$

and the periodic version (with the fourier transform defined in `fourier.md`):

$$
k^2 \tilde{V} = \frac{\tilde{\rho}}{\epsilon_0}
$$

we have

$$
\epsilon_0 = 8.8541878128(13) \times 10^{−12} \ \ \text{ F m}^{-1}
$$


$$
\epsilon_0 = 5.526349406 \times 10^{1} \ \ e^2 \ \text{eV}^{-1} \mu m^{-1}
$$


$$
\epsilon_0 = 5.526349406 \times 10^{-3} \ \ e^2 \ \text{eV}^{-1} \overset{\circ}{A} ^{-1}
$$


then, electrostatic energy:

$$
E = \frac{qV}{2}
$$
